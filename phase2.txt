## 1. General Medicine (Baseline / Default)

**Why:**
This becomes the *fallback* specialty and onboarding default for most clinics.

### Focus Areas

* Undifferentiated symptoms
* Early danger detection
* Chronic disease monitoring

### Key Risk Triggers

* Fever > 101°F for >3 days
* Weight loss >5% in 1 month
* Persistent pain >14 days
* Oxygen saturation <94%
* Polypharmacy (>5 meds)

### Context Lens

**Safety**

* Fever duration
* Oxygen status
* Red flag symptoms (night sweats, bleeding)

**Gaps**

* Pending labs
* Missed follow-ups
* Preventive screenings overdue

### SOAP Auto-Fields

* General Appearance
* Vitals Summary
* System-wise ROS checklist
* Provisional Dx + DDx

### Dashboard Accent

* Neutral Teal / Slate

## 2. Pediatrics

**Why:**
Completely different risk logic; age-based triage is critical.

### Focus Areas

* Growth & development
* Infection severity
* Parental red flags

### Key Risk Triggers

* Age <2 months + fever
* Poor feeding
* Lethargy / irritability
* Delayed milestones
* Respiratory rate above age-specific norm

### Context Lens

**Safety**

* Age-adjusted vitals
* Dehydration signs
* Immunization status

**Deltas**

* Growth percentile change
* Weight-for-age drop

### SOAP Auto-Fields

* Birth history
* Growth chart snapshot
* Developmental milestones
* Vaccination review

### Ordering Shortcuts

* CBC
* CRP
* Urine routine
* Chest X-ray (peds preset)

### Dashboard Accent

* Soft Blue / Mint

---

## 3. Orthopedics

**Why:**
Overlaps with Physio but focuses on **structural damage + surgical decisioning**.

### Focus Areas

* Trauma
* Structural deformities
* Neurovascular compromise

### Key Risk Triggers

* Suspected fracture
* Loss of sensation
* Loss of distal pulses
* Severe deformity
* Inability to bear weight

### Context Lens

**Safety**

* Neurovascular status
* Open vs closed injury

**Deltas**

* Pain progression
* Mobility loss

### SOAP Auto-Fields

* Mechanism of injury
* Site-specific exam
* Neurovascular exam
* ROM (active/passive)

### Ordering Shortcuts

* X-Ray (site specific)
* MRI (joint/spine)
* Immobilization order

### Dashboard Accent

* Steel Grey / Blue
